\section*{Chi-Squared Goodness-of-Fit Analysis}

The following figures present the results of the **Chi-Squared ($\chi^2$) goodness-of-fit test** applied to the positional data for the robot's motion. These tests assess how well the observed data distributions (for X and Y coordinates) conform to a theoretical distribution (e.g., a **Gaussian/Normal distribution** for the errors/residuals). The measurements are analyzed separately for the **OptiTrack** and **YouBot** sensing systems, across different motion profiles and payload masses.
