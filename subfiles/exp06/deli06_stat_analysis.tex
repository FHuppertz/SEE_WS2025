To find the accuracy and precision of the youBot robot arm, the gathered data has to be further analyzed. To find the accuracy the mean of the point clouds for each direction and object were compared with their respective ground truth. To find the precision a PCA was conducted (only on X and Y coordinates of the data) and the largest standard deviation is used to describe the worst case precision. The results of this are depicted in Tables \ref{tab:acc_prec_opti} and \ref{tab:acc_prec_rob}.
